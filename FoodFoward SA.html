Our Cause

Welcome to FoodFoward SA! We help fight hunger by redistributing surplus food. Our goal is to ensure that no food is put to waste and that every mouth possible is fed, beacause everybody deserves somthing to eat. FoodForward SA rescues surplus, nutritious food and redistributes it to charities and community programmes across South Africa. We work with retailers, manufacturers and farmers to ensure edible food reaches people rather than landfill. Together we reduce waste, fight hunger and support resilient communities.


Join us in our mission to make a difference in the lives of those in need.

How we help

  • Collect surplus food from businesses and farms
  • Sort and redistribute to registered charities
  • Support community kitchens, shelters and food gardens
  • Run education and food-security programmes
